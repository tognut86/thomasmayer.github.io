PERFORMANCE VISUAL ABOUT CONTACT

a live dance and music performance by Jelle de Witte, Francisco Martinez Miranda and Thomas Mayer.

Two bodies meet in a blue hour, where we feel lost, not knowing whether the day is about to break or to die out. It is a moment of transition. The beginning of a journey to move body and soul and to determine what is truly indispensable. Somewhere between light and darkness.

Created and performed for Festival CHI_CX20 and the Monopol Site Unseen Finissage, Berlin.
